Executive Summary

BharatVyb AI Solutions is an EdTech AI startup focused on developing cost-effective cloud AI ERPs for educational institutions, schools, and colleges. Based in Hyderabad, Telangana, the company aims to leverage AI and machine learning technologies to streamline administrative and educational processes. The initial phase will involve developing a prototype/POC while generating income through paid seminars and hackathons.

Vision and Mission

Vision: To revolutionize the educational sector with innovative AI-driven ERP solutions that enhance administrative efficiency and learning outcomes.

Mission: To develop and deploy AI-powered ERP systems tailored for educational institutions, while fostering AI/ML skills among students through seminars and hackathons.

BharatVyb AI Solutions addresses the challenge that many technically strong students face in India, where economic factors such as recession make it difficult for them to secure jobs. Our goal is to identify these bright students and provide them with opportunities to work for our AI company, developing SaaS products for the education domain.

Winners of our hackathons will be interviewed for internships at BharatVyb AI Solutions, where they will gain real-time experience. Interns who perform exceptionally well may be offered permanent positions. Our mission is to build a company powered by fresh talent, offering them the experience and opportunities they need to succeed.
